FIELD SALES ENGINEER

This technical sales position requires a highly motivated Field Sales Engineer (FSE) with specific experience in the mircrowave and RF industry.

The candidate must have the following abilities:
  • Identify and review engineering specifications
  • Coordinate and propose product configurations
  • Work with principles to negotiate, draw up and propose service and sales contracts
  • Work with OEM, CS and RF Design House on engineering, development and test projects
  • Self-starter, team player and a positive working attitude
  • Work independently from home
  • Travel locally 60%+ in the territories of South LA, Orange and San Diego Counties
The candidate must meet the following requirements:
  • BS and/or 5+ years sales and engineering experience in microwave design
  • Strong customer interface skills
  • Strong PC skills and proficient in MS Office software
  • Market knowledge in the microwave and RF industry including Telecom, Defense & Space, SATCOM, Broadcast Microwave and Cable TV preferred
  • Proficiency with CRM tools preferred
