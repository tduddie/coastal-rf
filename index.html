ABOUT COASTAL RF

WE ARE A FULL-SERVICE ELECTRONIC MANUFACTURERS’ REPRESENTATIVE COVERING ALL OF SOUTHERN CALIFORNIA. WITH OVER 40 YEARS OF EXPERIENCE IN RF & MICROWAVE DESIGN, WIRELESS NETWORK SYSTEMS ARCHITECTURE, AND SALES ENGINEERING, WE ARE ABLE TO PROVIDE SUPERIOR TECHNICAL SALES APPLICATIONS AND MARKETING SERVICES TO THE AEROSPACE, DEFENSE, TELECOM, AND COMMERCIAL/INDUSTRIAL ELECTRONICS INDUSTRIES FOR THE MANUFACTURERS THAT WE REPRESENT. WE PRIDE OURSELVES ON OUR ABILITY TO ACT AS “FIELD APPLICATIONS ENGINEERS” OR FAE’S FOR OUR COMPANIES IN ADDITION TO PROVIDING LOCAL SALES SUPPORT.

We are a customer oriented company that relies on strong professional relationships, technical capabilities, and cutting edge technology from manufacturers to meet our customer’s design needs and product development goals.

  • Jim is a Field Sales Engineer (FSE) covering the northern part of LA County as well as all of Ventura, Santa Barbara, San Luis Obispo, and Kern Counties. He has a proven FSE history in RF/Microwave and High Speed Digital/Optical applications working with several successful rep firms in California. Jim received a BSME from Iowa State University and MSEE from USC. His engineering experience came from working at Hughes Aircraft (now Raytheon Missile Systems) as a Guidance & Controls Engineer and TRW (Now Northrop Grumman Space) as a Sr. Systems Engineer.

  • Frank is President of Coastal RF Systems and Field Sales Engineer (FSE) for South LA, Orange, Riverside, San Diego and Imperial Counties. Frank founded Coastal RF Systems in 2011 after working in RF and Microwave Engineering for over 20 years. Prior Frank received his BSEE from Cal Poly Pomona and later completed several graduate courses in RF engineering from the University of Wisconsin and UCSD. His RF and Microwave engineering experience came from working at Powerwave Technologies Inc., where he held positions as Sr. Manager of Sales and Apps Engineering, Principal Systems Engineer and Sr. Design Engineer and also working at McCaw Cellular (Now AT&T Mobility) as a RF Engineer.

  • Rebecca started May of 2014 with Coastal RF Systems working as an Inside Sales Administrator which includes corresponding with our customers as well as our principals for quotes, samples, accounting data entry tasks. She brings 8+ years of experience in inside sales for the RF and Microwave Industry. Previously, she studied at University of Phoenix where she received an AA in Business Managements, a BS degree in Human Services, and a Master's degree in Psychology.
